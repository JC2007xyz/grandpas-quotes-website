Some of the funny tales from the life of a funny man

  1. Grandpa's new pants - Once grandpa had gotten himself a brand new pair of pants. Later that day a guy was trying to fight grandpa. Although grandpa is not one for physical violence he saw where it was going and prepared for battle by taking off his pants to keep them from getting dirty. For some odd reason the guy didn't want to fight pantsless grandpa and left.
