Grandpa's New Pants

Grandpa's new pants - Once grandpa had gotten himself a brand new pair of pants. Later that day a guy was trying to fight grandpa. Although grandpa is not one for physical violence he saw where it was going and prepared for battle by taking off his pants to keep them from getting dirty. For some odd reason the guy didn't want to fight pantsless grandpa and left.

"They were new."

2

Kabob Far And Wide

Whenever Grandpa is planning out a trip he likes to consider a lot of factors: Travel time, cost, must-see sights, and down time. However one of the most important factors for Grandpa is food. When most people go to a new place they like to test out the local cuisine. In Grandpa's mind the local cuisine is always kabob. He is known to look up kabob restaurants everywhere he travels without fail.

"Forget it that too expensive."

3

Poor Telemarketers

Grandma and Grandpa's home is a popular place for phone calls. They have spent a lifetime making friends and becoming well connected with family If you aren't greeted by Grandma's country voicemail greeting and are family then you will be greeted by Grandma or Grandpa. However if you are unfortunate enough to be a telemarketer calling, the phone will be picked up by Grandpa. However this version of Grandpa isn't the usual. This version has a special glint in his eyes, wind in his sails, and is far from normal. Sometimes when this version picks up he is 100 years old and lets his dog make his decisions. Other times he has several girlfriends. The telemarketers never have a normal experience when Grandpa picks up.

"Now they will not call again."

4

Drive Thru

When raising his 4 kids, Grandpa was met with many requests. One of these requests was to go to the drive thru. Finally obliging to the requests Grandpa pulled into a McDonald's. His kids were presumably happy because for some odd reason they thought they would be receiving food. Grandpa then drove thru the drive thru without ordering anything and left. He somewhat recently reenacted it with a nearby Dairy Queen drive thru.

"You say drive thru. I drive through."
