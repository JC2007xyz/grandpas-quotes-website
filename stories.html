Grandpa's New Pants

Grandpa's new pants - Once grandpa had gotten himself a brand new pair of pants. Later that day a guy was trying to fight grandpa. Although grandpa is not one for physical violence he saw where it was going and prepared for battle by taking off his pants to keep them from getting dirty. For some odd reason the guy didn't want to fight pantsless grandpa and left.

"They were new."

2

Kabob Far And Wide

Whenever Grandpa is planning out a trip he likes to consider a lot of factors: Travel time, cost, must-see sights, and down time. However one of the most important factors for Grandpa is food. When most people go to a new place they like to test out the local cuisine. In Grandpa's mind the local cuisine is always kabob. He is known to look up kabob restaurants everywhere he travels without fail.

"Forget it that too expensive."
