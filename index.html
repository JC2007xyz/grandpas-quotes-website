A bit about Grandpa

Grandpa was born in 1944 in Shiraz, Iran. He lived there for a while until he went to the University of Dayton to get his degree and meet his wife who goes by the name, Grandma. Together the two of them went to live in Tehran and have four kids. In the 80s, during the Iran-Iraq War, grandpa decided now was the time to test out his "green card marriage" and they moved back to Ohio. These days he has happily retired and likes to take several naps a day, watch videos/movies on the tv, see family, and drink cafe lattes.
