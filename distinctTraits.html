Distinct Traits

Some of the things people think of when they think about Grandpa

Home Babaisms Distinct Traits Stories Travels
  1. His height - Grandpa is notoriously short

  2. His driving speed - It is extremely rare to be in the car with grandpa and think "man we are going fast" or "we will arrive early" in fact many sources point towards the idea that such thoughts have never occured

  3. Cheap - Perhaps the word closest associated with grandpa. They say a dog is man's best friend, but for grandpa it is his wallet or maybe kabob.

  4. Defensive of his fridge - Food costs grandpa money and as we have learned earlier, that is highly valued. Therefore when hungry grandchildren who will eat anything in the fridge and only have an allergy to paying bills approach, it gives grandpa a run for his money. That is he literally will run to defend the fridge or steal the snacks back. He has even used a padlock on the fridge before.
