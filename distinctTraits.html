Distinct Traits

Some of the things people think of when they think about Grandpa

🧍Height(or lack of it)

Grandpa is notoriously short. Often when we can't find Grandpa we have forgotten to look down.

🚗Driving speed

It is extremely rare to be in the car with Grandpa and think "Man we are going fast." or "We will arrive early." in fact many sources point towards the idea that such thoughts have never occurred.

💰Cheapness

Perhaps the word closest associated with Grandpa. They say a dog is man's best friend, but for Grandpa it is his wallet or maybe kabob.

🛡️Defensive of his fridge

Food costs Grandpa money and as we have learned earlier, that is highly valued. Therefore when hungry grandchildren who will eat anything in the fridge and only have an allergy to paying bills approach, it gives Grandpa a run for his money. That is he literally will run to defend the fridge or steal the snacks back. He has even used a padlock on the fridge before.

🍰Sweet Tooth

Grandpa has had diabetes for several years, but not once during that time has anyone questioned how he got it. His house always contains multiple flavors of ice cream and cookies. He can often be found in the kitchen cooking up a new sweet.

👨‍🍳Chef

Kitchens cost a lot of money and as we have discussed, Grandpa is cheap. Therefore he must make frequent use of it to get his money's worth. Grandpa has made his own yogurts and Grandma makes jellies to go with them. They frequently cook up delicious meals together that attract the whole family. Their home is a place of most family gatherings.
